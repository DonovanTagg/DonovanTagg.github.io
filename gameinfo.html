How to play

At the begining of a combat, unless by the Game Masters discresion, the players begin. You normally have 3 types of actions. One move, one ability and one anytime. As a viewer, move is represented by a green diamon, ability by a blue diamond and anytime red diamond with an exclamation point. There are some circumstances, like the Monk which replaces all their actions with anytimes or a Puppeter who has 3 chaos actions with Psychic Strings. A chaos action does exactly what it says. It can cause chaos. It lets you use any of the three actions above. So lets say you are a Kobold Seaswagger Monk with spirit rush. You cast spirt rush which causes you to gain 5 chaos actions. That means you can use 5 actions, anytimes, or movement in any order with no limits.
