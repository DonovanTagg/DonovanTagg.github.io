The Game

Urealms live is a RPG game that takes many ideas from D&D, while changing and adding their own twist. An easy description would be calling it a simplified D&D. That does not mean it is a basic game, as it has its own ruelset, items and classes that are unique to the game. It is commonly played on a game called Tabletop Simulator and the creator of the game, Rob, has importated all the cards and parts of the game to Tabletop.
